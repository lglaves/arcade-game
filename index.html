Help Buddy Escape to the Sea

How to Play

Level 1 Play

400 points will get you to the next level of the game. Advance playing character to the water tiles to gain points.

  • Move the playing character by pressing the keyboard Arrow Keys Up, Down, Left or Right.
  • Pause the game by pressing the space key, but note that this will not pause the countdown timer.
  • Colliding with a bug causes you to lose 50 points and be returned to start row.
  • Reaching the water gives you an extra 50 points and returns you to start row.

Level 2 Play

800 points wins.

  • In Level 2 you gain 50 points for catching a gem and 100 points for catching a heart.
  • In Level 2 you lose 50 points for bug collisions.
